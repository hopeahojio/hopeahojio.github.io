Malala Yousafazai

Some of those attributes of Malala which I admire include:

  • Perseverance - Even in the face of enormous difficulty, she continued
  • Passion - By speaking out on topics that need attention and awareness, she engages in activism for female education
  • Braveness - For the sake of other people, she speaks out to help bring forth justice and inclusion
  • Leaving a legacy - Because of the efforts put forth, she is paving the way for others

Malala Yousafazai, even from a young age, faced numerous obstacles, but persevered. She exemplifies everything that a hero could be.

What truly makes a hero is the attributes that they embody, even through the most difficult circumstances.
